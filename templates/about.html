About

This is python chatbot that uses deep learning to predict answers for frequently asked questions by guests at hotels.

As a reference the FAQs published by radisson hotel group at their website are used. These include questions about hotel policies, reservations, customer support and travel plans.

https://www.radissonhotels.com/en-us/faq

The feedback page provides the option of sending a feedback message to the developer.

This Chatbot is developed as part of an assignment for CETM 46 module in the MSc data science course at university of Sunderland.
